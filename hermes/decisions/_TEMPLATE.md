## Context

## Decision

## Alternatives Considered

What other options were on the table? For each, briefly explain why it was rejected.

This section matters more than the decision itself. The decision is what we did. The alternatives are why we didn't do other things, which is often what future readers need to know.

### Positive

### Negative
- What gets worse, or what tradeoffs we accept.
- What complexity or cost we now own.

### Neutral
- Effects that are notable but neither good nor bad.
